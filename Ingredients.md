# INGREDIENTS YOU WILL NEED TO MAKE SUGAR COOKIES
1. *Butter*: Sweet Cream Salted butter, softened to room temperature.
2. *Sugars*: You will need Granulated Sugar for the cookie dough
3. *Egg*: Large, room temperature eggs.
4. *Vanilla Extract*: vanilla makes everything taste better.
5. *Baking Powder*: to help create a soft cookie that keeps its shape and has a little bit of rise.
6. *Flour*: We use All-Purpose Flour for these cookies.
